<!-- MODERN TEACHER PORTAL DESIGN GUIDE -->
<!-- Apply these styles to the teacher portal -->

DESIGN PRINCIPLES:
1. Clean white backgrounds with subtle gray accents
2. Professional blue color scheme (#0066CC primary, #003D7A dark)
3. Card-based layout with subtle shadows
4. Clear typography hierarchy
5. Minimal use of emojis, focus on icons
6. Generous whitespace
7. Smooth transitions

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary: #0066CC (Professional Blue)
- Dark: #003D7A (Navy)
- Success: #00A651 (Green)
- Warning: #FF9900 (Orange)
- Danger: #CC0000 (Red)
- Gray: #F5F5F5 (Background)
- White: #FFFFFF (Cards)

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Headings: Montserrat, Bold
- Body: Open Sans, Regular
- Buttons: Uppercase, Bold, Letter-spacing

COMPONENTS:
- Navigation: Horizontal tabs with underline indicator
- Cards: White background, subtle shadow, rounded corners
- Buttons: Solid colors, uppercase text
- Forms: Clean inputs with focus states
- Tables: Striped rows, hover effects
